About Cricket Sport

Cricket is a bat-and-ball sport played between two teams of eleven players on a field with a 22-yard pitch. Known as the "gentleman's game," cricket has evolved from its English origins to become one of the world's most popular sports, especially in countries like India, Australia, England, Pakistan, and South Africa.

Test Cricket

The traditional and longest format

  • • 5 days maximum
  • • 2 innings per team
  • • No over limit
  • • Red ball used

ODI Cricket

One Day International format

  • • 50 overs per team
  • • 1 innings per team
  • • 8 hours duration
  • • White ball used

T20 Cricket

Twenty20 - fastest format

  • • 20 overs per team
  • • 1 innings per team
  • • 3 hours duration
  • • White ball used
